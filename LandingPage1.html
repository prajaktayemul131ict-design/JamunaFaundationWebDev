About Us

At Jamuna Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering students and individuals through various initiatives aimed at fostering self-employment and educational growth. Our training and internship programs are designed to equip students and individuals with the skills they need to thrive. We actively organize blood donation drives, medical diagnostic camps, and provide free legal advice to ensure that justice is accessible to all. Join us in our mission to combat pollution, promote tree plantation, and educate communities against the dangers of drugs and alcohol.
